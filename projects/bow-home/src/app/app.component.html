bow.finance (BOW) uses an automatic market maker mechanism to serve the Heco chain stablecoin flash swap platform

Description

Bow.finance is an open-source, automated market maker (AMM) mechanism designed to enable token swaps between stablecoins on Huobi ECO Chain (Heco), which ensures our swaps have low fees and minimal price slippage. Combining efficient, decentralized stablecoin swaps on Heco with multi-stablecoin “savings accounts, bow.finance also lets users add funds to the protocol’s liquidity to earn interest.

Bow.finance will launch BOW, a governance token that will allow holders to decide on bow.finance's future, in 2021.
